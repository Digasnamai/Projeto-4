PERCEPTA


            

                

                    
About Section

                    
Using the theme "Technology and Society," specifically techno-anxiety, surveillance, hybrid
                        intelligence, and automation, our goal is to create an interactive experience where a simulated
                        work environment is the main component. This multimedia object aims to incite critical thinking.
                        By creating a charismatic virtual assistant that mimics human behavior and distracts the user,
                        we highlight the negative impacts of technological evolution and the excessive use of artificial
                        intelligence. Thus, we emphasize the inconsequentiality of isolated individual rejection and the
                        urgency of a collective effort; it is not enough for one person to reject this technology to
                        solve the problem; a joint movement is necessary to minimize social and environmental damage.
